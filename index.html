Nearby Attractions

Shangarh Meadows

A visit to Shangarh meadows will leave you breathless: they are large, lush green, calm, and beautiful.

Barshangarh Waterfall

Reached by a short drive and then a hike of half a kilometre, the Barshangarh Falls are a pleasant delight on this mountainous terrain

Raila Fort & Waterfall

Drive down for a short tour, covering the twin towers, the village of Raila, the Raila Temple and the hidden waterfalls

Thini Thatch & Pundrik Lake

The Great Himalayan National Park offers many mesmerizing trekking trails for you to traverse

Explore nearby villages

Plan a visit to the nearby villages, surrounded by apple orchards and the famous wooden Himachali house.

Bird watching at GHNP

Shangarh, is home to a variety of native birds that welcome travellers. GHNP serves as a habitat for nearly 100 different bird species

Travel to Tirthan Valley

The magnificent Tirthan Valley is only a two-hour drive from Shangarh and well worth a visit. Gushaini and Sarchi are breathtakingly beautiful places located along the Tirthan River

Jalori Pass & Jibhi

Plan a trek to Raghupur Fort & Sereolsar Lake. This trek will take you through the dramatic high altitude landscapes. Also explore the lush green landscapes and waterfalls at Jibhi

How to Reach

By air: The closest airport to Shangarh is the Bhuntar Airport at approximately 47 km. You can get a bus or hire a cab for the further commute.
By train: The closest railway station is Chandigarh Railway Station. It is approximately 250 km away. You can get a bus or hire a cab for the further commute.
By bus: Firstly, you will have to reach Aut for whichyou can take any bus going towards Kullu, Kasol and Manali. You can get a bus or hire a cab for the further commute.
By car: If you are taking your vehicle or hiring a cab, there are two options. You can either choose the way via Shimla or Kiratpur.
